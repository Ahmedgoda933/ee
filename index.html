About me

Hello! I'M Ahmed EL-Kilany

Hello! My name is Ahmed Al Kilany and I am a designer currently based in Egypt. I have a passion for printmaking and have had the privilege of studying under some of the most respected professionals in the field, gaining a great deal of knowledge when it comes to digital and analog design practices. I love experimenting with different mediums and constantly learning new artistic styles. I have deep understanding of most digital design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Indesign and am well versed in hand lettering, large format murals, screen printing, block printing, letterpress, collage and apparel design. I also enjoy facilitating workshops and working on community art projects. Feel free to contact me for any upcoming projects and I would love to work with you!
